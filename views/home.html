OUR MISSION

GreenSpeak was created by four high school students to increase environmental and political activism. By streamlining the process of gathering and identifying environmental issues, we hope to be a catalyst for involvement and proactivity in the larger worldwide Environmental Movement. Here, you can educate yourself on environmental statistics and current events, and take action! Our site makes getting involved easier than ever, and individuals can do so in their own unique ways.

WHY DOES IT MATTER?

Climate Change and environmental issue, despite arguably being the most prominent issue in the modern world, is not the most addressed.

In a 2023 political study, Americans ranked "dealing with climate change" as the 17th most important issue to be addressed out of 21.
